WHO ARE WE?

Conductor Rosy Ghorra


Lebanese


From Zahle, Rosy Ghorra is a musicologist (USEK) working in musical education, and having founded Anqoud along with a group of young adults in 2017.

Our choir is composed of over 60 singers whose ages vary from 9 to 99 singing different ranges.

Initiatives


Part of Beirut Bi Alb Zahle Initiative, Anqoud is not just a singing choir but an active social group trying to draw a smile on people's faces.
